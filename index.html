About Pune

Pune is one of the most vibrant cities in Maharashtra. It is known for its rich history, educational institutions, pleasant climate and cultural heritage. Pune has many historical places, temples, forts and modern attractions that make it a popular tourist destination.

Famous Places in Pune

Shaniwar Wada

A historic fort built by the Peshwas in the 18th century.

Sinhagad Fort

A famous trekking destination with beautiful views.

Aga Khan Palace

An important landmark connected with India's freedom movement.
