Top Places to Visit in Pune

Discover some of the most famous tourist attractions in Pune.

Shaniwar Wada

A historic fortification in Pune built in 1732 by the Peshwas. It is one of the most important landmarks in the city.

Sinhagad Fort

A famous hill fort located near Pune. It is popular for trekking, historic battles and breathtaking views.

Aga Khan Palace

A beautiful palace built in 1892 and an important site connected to India's freedom struggle.

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple

One of the most famous temples in Pune dedicated to Lord Ganesha and visited by thousands of devotees every year.
